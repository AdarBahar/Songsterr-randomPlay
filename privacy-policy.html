Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2025

Data Collection and Usage

The Random Favorite Picker extension for Songsterr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser and only interacts with Songsterr.com when you're actively using the site.

Storage

The extension stores only two pieces of information locally in your browser:

  • Your preferred keyboard shortcut key
  • Debug mode setting (enabled/disabled)

This information is stored using Chrome's built-in storage system and can be synchronized across your devices if you use Chrome Sync.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your preferences
  • activeTab: To interact with Songsterr.com
  • scripting: To add the random song functionality
  • tabs: To detect when you're on Songsterr.com

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics.
