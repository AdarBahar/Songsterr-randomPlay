Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Data Collection and Usage

The Random Favorite Picker extension for Songsterr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser and only interacts with Songsterr.com when you're actively using the site.

Storage

The extension stores only your preferences locally in your browser:

  • Your preferred keyboard shortcut key
  • Your preferred instrument (Default, Guitar, Bass, or Drums)
  • Your randomization settings (mode and the newness / least-played weights)
  • Debug mode setting (enabled/disabled)

This information is stored using Chrome's built-in storage system and can be synchronized across your devices if you use Chrome Sync. To pick a random song, the extension reads your favorites from Songsterr's API using your existing login, inside your browser; this is used only to choose a song and is never stored long-term or sent to any third party.

Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your keyboard shortcut preference and debug mode setting locally in your browser

The extension also uses content scripts that run only on Songsterr.com pages to add the random song button and functionality. No additional permissions are required for this.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics.
